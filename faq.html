FOSS Meet 24 at NITC - Frequently Asked Questions

FOSS Meet 24 is the 24th edition of the Free and Open Source Software conference organized by NITC. It brings together enthusiasts, developers, and professionals to discuss the latest trends, technologies, and projects in the open-source community.
FOSS Meet 24 is scheduled to be held from June 15-17, 2024 at the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC) campus, located in Calicut, Kerala.
The event is open to students, developers, open-source contributors, and anyone interested in free and open-source technologies. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's something for everyone.
